### 🎯 LEETCODE BLOCK (60 min, every day 6:00–7:00 PM)

**Daily target: 2 problems.** That's it.

**Process per problem (~25–30 min each):**
1. Read the problem 2x. Don't code yet.
2. Watch NeetCode's video explanation BEFORE attempting (this week only — we'll wean you off in Week 3).
3. Close the video. Code from scratch in your editor.
4. If stuck after 20 min, look at the solution, understand it, then close it and rewrite.
5. Log in your tracker: name, time, did-I-need-help.

Why 60 min for 2 problems: at your baseline (~5 lifetime), each problem will genuinely take 25–30 min including the video + your attempt + logging. By Week 4 you'll do 2 in 40 min. Don't rush this week.
